"Victoria Loschuk cleverly uses focus as a physical, visual and conceptual tool to create subtle spatial uncertainty and an eerie luminosity. Shapes, forms and hues slowly become 'nature in the city' views, which simultaneously pay homage to the lengthy landscape tradition and reflect on its postmodern significance."
        — Irina D. Costache, Curator
